# The Untainted Heart

> :memo: **note:** Base text for AI story engine

> :memo: **note:** This is not the code for the AI story engine itself - this repo contains the files (markdown files) that the engine will read to maintain a specific story - the Untainted Heart, in this case. To see the engine in action, visit [Fable Facet](http://fablefacet.com/ffacet.mesis.html). (still in development, not released)<br/>

This story is open access - all rights, copyrights, and royalties are waived - and this is also loosely based on a inspirational piece I read more than 30 years ago. I've tried to identify the source material but have been unable to - so if you have this info, please drop me a line (but all I can remember from it is the very basic idea: people running through a radioactive, post-apocalyptic desert in tanks searching for a legend of a machine that could render them immune to radiation - I don't remember how they called the machine - and one of them finds a woman who seems to be immune).<br/> 

The entire world is a grey desert, or at least it is what the characters believe (I don't recall the color mentioned in the source text). Radiation is so high it is impossible to be on the surface without an anti-rad suit. There is no explanation about what caused this.<br/>

The sand crawlers are habitats, environments, where the dusters live for the long span of their runs. They are also tanks and cargo trucks. The tech is heavily based on sand - sand cannons, sandcrete, etc.<br/>

If you don't mind spoilers, the blueprint.md provides more details about the intention of the text and how the AI will try to drive the action.<br/>

If you are reading this on Fable Facet, the source files are on Github, https://github.com/casson-projects-2020/untainted-heart/. Feel free to open a discussion or an issue there.<br/>
